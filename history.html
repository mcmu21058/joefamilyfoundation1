History

The founders of the Joe Family Foundation desire to create a space for African American families including people of all diverse backgrounds. That has experienced a life-changing event. After personally experiencing a traumatic life event that changed their family forever. Through these initiatives, the foundation will work in advocacy to improve health & racial equity in healthcare and the disability community.

Current COVID-19 Initivies

  3. Stay Home Safe Care Packages

Services: Our four core services are:
