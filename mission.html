Mission

Our Mission: To be a catalyst of change for minority youth with disabilities. Supporting the youth and their loved ones, by providing a trusted platform for networking. Ensuring that no matter the disability or racial ethnicity, life goals can be achieved through education, advocacy, and partnership building. Bringing light to the unique issues faced daily by youth of color with disabilities and their loved ones.
